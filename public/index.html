Gmail IP Analyzer

Extract sender IPs from emails and look up location & ISP via ip-api.com

Connect & Analyze

Use your Gmail address and an App Password (not your normal password). Unread emails in your Spam folder will be processed and marked as read.

Results

Sender IP is parsed from Received: headers (e.g. Received: from host (name [185.174.29.12])). Data from ip-api.com (45 req/min).
